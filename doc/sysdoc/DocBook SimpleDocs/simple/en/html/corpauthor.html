corpauthor — A corporate author, as opposed to an individual

Synopsis

Mixed Content Model

corpauthor ::=
(#PCDATA|link|ulink|emphasis|trademark|replaceable|
 inlinemediaobject)*

Attributes

Common attributes

Description

In documents that have no specific authors, but are credited as authored by a corporation, the CorpAuthor tag can be used in place of the Author tag to indicate authorship. This element is used in bibliographic metadata.

Processing expectations

May be formatted inline or as a displayed block, depending on context. Sometimes suppressed.

Parents

These elements contain corpauthor: articleinfo, authorgroup, bibliomixed, bibliomset, objectinfo, sectioninfo, subtitle, title, titleabbrev.

Children

The following elements occur in corpauthor: emphasis, inlinemediaobject, link, replaceable, trademark, ulink.

See Also

author, authorblurb, authorgroup, editor, othercredit.
