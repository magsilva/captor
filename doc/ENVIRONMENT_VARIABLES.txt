
Environment variable setting configuration manual

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Windows XP:


	- Click with rigth-button on "My Computer" desktop icon.
	- Click in the "Advanced" tab.
	- Click in "Environment variables" button.	
	- Click in "New" button.
	- Put the name and the value of the new variable.
	- Click "OK" button.
	- Restart all opened DOS prompt.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Linux:

	- In the "bash" execute the following command:

	$export variable_name='variable_value'
